Overview

InfiniTD is a work-in-progress incremental, multiplayer tower defense game. It is the work of a single, hobbyist developer and is currently far from a finished game. Expect significant changes and occasional data wipes as development continues.

The general idea is it’s a tower defense game where you choose not only which towers to place where, but also which enemies will make up the waves. Your towers constantly face waves of enemies, even when you’re away meaning you’ll always have more gold when you come back. It’s impossible to lose, but how quickly you progress will be determined by how well you play. In addition to facing the waves you’ve designed you’ll also face the waves of players nearby on the leaderboard, making optimal play depend on how they’ve designed their defenses and waves (Note: This multiplayer aspect is coming soon).

How to Play

Make an account by clicking on the account tab and logging in with one of Google, Facebook, or Github.

Next, select the “My Battleground” tab to see your battleground. This is where you’ll build your towers and watch them face waves of enemies.

To build a new tower open the build tab on the right and choose a tower you can afford. Next, select somewhere to place it. You can place a tower on any empty square that won’t separate the enemy spawn (the green circle) from the enemy goal (the red circle). The dirt path shows how enemies will travel to the goal. When you select a place for a tower to go you’ll see a representation of its range and firing rate. To actually build the tower click the build button or press ‘b’.

To get more gold you’ll have to defeat enemies. To do this open the wave tab and add enemies to a wave. The more enemies you can defeat the faster your gold will grow. To test out the wave go to the Battle tab and select start battle or press the space bar.

Once you finish a battle your gold rate will be updated based on the results. Every minute your gold will grow based on this gold rate.

In the future there will be multiplayer features, but those aren’t available yet.

Data Deletion

All account information (but not logs) can be deleted by going to account tab and clicking the “Delete Account” button.
